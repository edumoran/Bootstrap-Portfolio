Eduardo Morán
Digital Solutions Designer + Story Teller
I'm a passionate problem solver and professional storyteller by trade. I believe that design is not an output but the process that leads to it. It's all about telling a good story.

Owning and developing new projects is what really makes me tick. Taking an idea and pairing it up with tons of research, as well as personal and cultural references. Adding experimentention and iteration will definately turn these into an exciting finished product.

Creating high-quality products and long-lasting relationships lies in the true understanding of the customer. It all comes down to finding the details that when put together will create an amazing story.

WHAT DRIVES ME
PRODUCT INNOVATION AND DEVELOPMENT

Creating digital products by carefully combining research, design, iteration, testing and continuous client-designer communication.

DATA RESEARCH, ANALYSIS AND VISUALIZATION

Inspecting, cleansing, transforming, and visualizing large amounts of data and turning it into visual and emotion-triggering stories.

CORPORATE IDENTITY CREATION AND IMPLEMENTATION

Examining and translating the defining idea and the reason to be of the companies into visual, coherent, and relatable language.
